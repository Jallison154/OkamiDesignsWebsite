About the LED Video Wall Calculator

The Okami Designs LED video wall calculator helps production teams, integrators, and designers plan real-world LED walls before equipment hits the truck. Enter cabinet size and pixel pitch, set your wall grid, and the tool calculates total resolution, physical dimensions, and how your content will fit on screen.

Use it as an LED wall resolution calculator to see pixel count per cabinet and for the full wall, or as an LED wall layout calculator when you are building wide and tall arrays from standard modules. Processor planning is built in: port capacity, fill threshold, and estimated circuit load update as you change the design.

The processor port calculator shows how many outputs you need based on pixel load and safe headroom—useful when specing NovaStar, Colorlight, or similar systems alongside your wall design. A video wall aspect ratio overlay helps you check 16:9 and other formats against the native wall shape so you know where letterboxing will appear.

Download a PDF build sheet for install reference, save projects locally, and iterate quickly during pre-production. The calculator is free to use in the browser with no account required.
